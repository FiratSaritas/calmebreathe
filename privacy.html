Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 11, 2025

At Calm Breathe, your privacy is of utmost importance to us. This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, and protect any information you provide while using our website.

1. Information We Collect

We are committed to providing a privacy-focused experience. As such, Calm Breathe does not collect any personal identifiable information (PII) from its users. We do not require registration, collect names, email addresses, IP addresses, or any other data that could be used to identify you personally.

When you use our site, your choices (e.g., selected mood, environment, duration) are processed locally within your browser to facilitate the session. This information is not stored on our servers or shared with third parties.

2. Analytics and Usage Data

We may use anonymous, aggregated usage data (e.g., number of sessions started, most popular choices) for internal analytical purposes to understand how the service is used and to improve it. This data is entirely anonymous and cannot be linked back to individual users.

3. Cookies

Calm Breathe uses minimal cookies, primarily for functional purposes (e.g., to remember your last selections locally for a smoother user experience if you revisit). These cookies do not contain personal information and are not used for tracking across other websites.

Third-party services, such as Google AdSense, may use cookies. Please refer to their respective privacy policies for more information:

  • Google Privacy Policy
  • How Google uses information from sites or apps that use our services

4. Third-Party Services

Our website uses Google AdSense to display advertisements. Google, as a third-party vendor, uses cookies to serve ads on our site. Google's use of the DART cookie enables it to serve ads to our users based on their visit to our site and other sites on the Internet. Users may opt out of the use of the DART cookie by visiting the Google Ad and Content Network Privacy Policy.

We are not responsible for the privacy practices of external sites linked from Calm Breathe.

5. Data Security

We implement reasonable security measures to protect the integrity and security of our website. However, no method of transmission over the Internet or method of electronic storage is 100% secure. While we strive to use commercially acceptable means to protect your information, we cannot guarantee its absolute security.

6. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last updated" date. You are advised to review this Privacy Policy periodically for any changes.

7. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us: your.email@example.com or visit our Contact Us page.
